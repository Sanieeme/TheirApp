About This Program

The Bono Foundation Tutoring Program is focused on improving academic performance for students who need extra support or want to excel further in their studies.

We match students with tutors who specialise in specific subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and more. Sessions are designed to be interactive, simple, and focused on understanding concepts rather than memorisation.

Personalised Learning

Lessons are adapted to your pace and learning style.

Exam Preparation

Focused revision sessions to help you perform better in exams.

Subject Mastery

Break down difficult topics into simple, understandable concepts.

Confidence Building

Improve your confidence in tackling academic challenges.
