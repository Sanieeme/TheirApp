About Bono Foundation

Empowering youth through mentorship, education, and career development.

Who We Are

Bono Foundation is a youth development organisation focused on empowering young people with the skills, confidence, and guidance they need to succeed in life.

We believe that every young person deserves access to quality mentorship, academic support, and career guidance regardless of their background.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between potential and opportunity by providing mentorship, tutoring, and career guidance to students and young professionals.

Our Vision

To build a generation of confident, skilled, and purpose-driven young leaders across Africa and beyond.
