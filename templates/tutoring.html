Our Tutoring Program

Bono Foundation’s tutoring program connects students with skilled tutors to enhance academic performance. Whether you're struggling in a subject or looking to excel further, we aim to help you achieve your educational goals.

What You’ll Benefit From

  • One-on-one personalized tutoring
  • Help with homework, assignments, and exam prep
  • Improved understanding of key concepts
  • Regular progress tracking
